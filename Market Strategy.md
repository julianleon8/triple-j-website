# Triple J Metal LLC — Market Strategy

## Pricing
- Triple J welded 20x20 carport: **$4,500 – $7,500**
- Alan's Factory Outlet (bolted kit): $1,795
- Texas Metal Buildings CTX (bolted): $2,200 – $3,500
- Leland's: carports from $2,000, RV covers $4,000, garages $6,000, barns $13,000, barndo shells $20,000
- Peak Steel (commercial red iron): $8,000 – $12,000 for 20x20

## Target Markets

### 1. Luxury Subdivisions (KTB Corridor)
- Builders: Saratoga Homes, Flintrock Building Texas
- Developments: Heritage Oaks, Bella Charca
- Home prices: $500k – $900k
- Opportunity: Builders only build the primary residence — Triple J owns the secondary structure market (RV covers, premium porches, garages)
- Key angle: HOA-compliant architectural panels (Board & Batten, Standing Seam)
- Key angle: Turnkey concrete — wealthy buyers do NOT want to manage multiple contractors
- Key angle: 48-hr builds = low disruption to neighborhood

### 2. Fort Cavazos Military Families
- Location: 10 minutes from Heritage Oaks
- Demographic: Transient — PCSing in, need immediate vehicle/property protection
- Key angle: 48-hour turnaround (can't wait 12 weeks like competitors)
- Key angle: Military discount + military language (PCS, BAH, VA Loans)
- Marketing: Facebook geofence ads to Killeen-Temple-Belton corridor

## Key Competitive Advantages
1. **48-hour build time** — East Texas Carports takes 4–16 weeks
2. **Turnkey concrete + site prep** — no competitor includes this
3. **Welded not bolted** — permanent real estate improvement, doesn't rattle in storms
4. **Local** — Temple-based, same-week scheduling
5. **No middlemen** — one call, one company, start to finish

## Competitor Weaknesses to Exploit
- **East Texas Carports:** 4–16 week lead times, excludes site prep, extra fees for customizations
- **Alan's Factory Outlet:** Cheap bolted kits, customer handles own site prep + permits
- **Safeguard Metal Buildings (Waco):** Warranties voided if land not perfectly level, zero concrete services
- **Mueller Inc.:** Rigid sales policies, customers must offload own materials, impersonal
- **Leland's Metal Buildings:** High-volume mass production, not custom/bespoke
