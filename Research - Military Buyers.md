# Military Buyer Research — Fort Cavazos / Triple J Metal LLC
*Last updated: 2026-04-15 via NotebookLM*

## Market Profile
- **Fort Cavazos** is ~10 minutes from Heritage Oaks (luxury subdivision in Killeen)
- Generates a high-volume, transient residential market
- Military families arrive in waves tied to PCS (Permanent Change of Station) orders

## Purchase Drivers
- **Urgency:** PCS timelines are fixed — families need immediate weather protection for vehicles upon arrival
- **Budget framework:** Purchases are sized against BAH (Basic Allowance for Housing) and VA loan capacity for Bell County
- **PCS timing:** Peak season is spring/summer — plan campaigns and inventory accordingly

## Messaging Hooks That Convert
- **48-hour build speed** — the single most powerful hook for this demographic
- "Same week as site prep approval"
- "We work with your PCS timeline"
- Military and first-responder **discount** prominently in CTA
- Terms to weave in naturally: "PCSing," "BAH," "VA Loans" — these signal that Triple J understands the military lifestyle
- Emphasize permanence: a welded carport adds **real estate value** vs. a kit structure (relevant for families who may sell/rent when they PCS out)

## Channel Strategy
- **Geofenced Facebook/Instagram ads** targeting Fort Cavazos zip codes and surrounding Killeen/Harker Heights zip codes
- Facebook groups: "Fort Cavazos Families," "PCS to Fort Cavazos," Bell County military spouse groups
- **Timing:** Ramp ad spend January–April ahead of summer PCS wave

## Objection Handling
- "I might PCS in 3 years" → Welded structure adds resale value, renters prefer covered parking, recoverable as capital improvement
- "I can't afford it" → Financing options, BAH offsets, military discount
- "I don't need it yet" → Weather in Central TX (hail, heat, UV) damages vehicles fast; same-week build means no long commitment needed
