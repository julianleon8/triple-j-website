# Triple J Metal LLC — SEO Strategy

## Business Overview
- **Company:** Triple J Metal LLC
- **Location:** Central Texas (primary), Dallas + Austin (secondary)
- **Specialty:** Residential carports — welded and bolted red iron steel
- **Positioning:** Cheapest + best carport builders in Central Texas

## Competitive Positioning

**Triple J wins on:**
1. **Price** — explicitly cheapest in Central Texas
2. **Local** — not a kit company, real local installers
3. **Both options** — welded AND bolted (more than most local competitors)
4. **Quality materials** — named suppliers (MetalMax, MetalMart — trust signal)

**Differentiation message:**
> "Central Texas's most affordable metal carport builder. Welded or bolted red iron — installed right, priced right."

---

## Content Pillars

1. **Carport Types & Materials** — welded vs bolted, red iron explained, sizing guide
2. **Local Service Areas** — city-specific pages for every target market
3. **Pricing Transparency** — ballpark pricing, what factors affect cost
4. **Installation Process** — what to expect, how long it takes, permits
5. **Social Proof** — photo gallery, reviews, completed jobs
