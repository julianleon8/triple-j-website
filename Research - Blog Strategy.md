# Blog Content Strategy — Triple J Metal LLC
*Last updated: 2026-04-15 via NotebookLM*

## Core Principle
Over-communicate Triple J's **specific Temple, Texas location** in every post. This proactively distances the brand from poorly-reviewed businesses with similar names:
- "Triple J Custom Welding" — San Antonio, BBB complaints for unfulfilled BBQ grills
- "Triple J Metal Buildings LLC" — Henderson TX

Every post should include the city, county, and/or neighborhood in the first paragraph and at least one subheading.

## Post Format Standard
**Structure each post as a detailed case study or local guide:**
1. Hook — local problem statement (e.g., "Bell County hail season," "Fort Cavazos PCS wave")
2. What homeowners need to know — research-backed data
3. Triple J's approach — specific materials, specs, process
4. ComparisonTable — Triple J vs competitors on the key axis
5. Local CTA — "Get a free quote from our Temple TX crew"

**Project gallery integration:**
- Feature "Before/After" photos in each post where possible
- Show skid steer performing site prep → final welded structure
- These photos also feed GBP posting strategy

## SEO Technical Requirements Per Post
- `@type: BlogPosting` JSON-LD with `datePublished`, `author`, `publisher`
- `alternates.canonical` = `/blog/[slug]`
- Target keyword in: title, H1, first paragraph, at least one subheading
- Internal links: each post links to the quote form and at least one service/location page

## Publishing Cadence
- Tier 1: publish as built
- Tier 2: 1 per month after Tier 1 complete
- Each new post = new GBP photo post linking to the article
