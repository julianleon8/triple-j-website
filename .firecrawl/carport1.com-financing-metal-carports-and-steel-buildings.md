## Frequently Asked Questions

Does applying for financing affect my credit score?

It depends on the program. Our rent-to-own (RTO) options through RTO National, EZ Pay, and Heartland do **not** run a credit check, so your score is not affected at all. Traditional financing through GreenSky does require a credit check, which may result in a soft or hard inquiry depending on the stage of the application. We will always let you know upfront before anything hits your credit.

Can I pay off my building early?

Yes. All of our RTO programs have **no early payoff penalties**. You can pay ahead, pay extra each month, or pay off the full balance at any time. For traditional financing through GreenSky, early payoff terms depend on your specific agreement — ask your representative for details.

What documents do I need to apply?

For RTO programs, you typically need a valid government-issued ID and proof of income (a recent pay stub or bank statement). There is no extensive paperwork. For traditional financing, you may also need your Social Security number and basic employment information. The entire application usually takes less than 10 minutes.

How long after approval until my building is installed?

Once your financing is approved and your order is confirmed, typical lead times are **4 to 8 weeks** depending on your building's size and complexity. Installation itself is usually completed in a single day for standard structures, or two to three days for larger commercial buildings. Your site needs to be level and accessible before our crew arrives.

Can I finance a custom-designed building?

Absolutely. Financing applies to every building we sell, including fully custom configurations. Choose your dimensions, roof style, colors, door placements, windows, and accessories — then finance the total. You can even use our [3D configurator](https://design.carport1.com/) to design your building and then apply for financing on the final price.

What is the difference between rent-to-own and traditional financing?

With **rent-to-own (RTO)**, you make monthly payments without a credit check, and you own the building once all payments are complete. The building can technically be returned if your situation changes. With **traditional financing**, you own the building immediately and make loan payments to a lender. Traditional financing typically offers lower overall cost but requires a credit check and approval.

Is delivery and installation really free?

Yes. Every metal building from Carport1 includes **free delivery and professional installation** at your prepared site. This applies whether you pay in full, use RTO, or finance through one of our lending partners. No hidden fees, no surprise charges at delivery.
