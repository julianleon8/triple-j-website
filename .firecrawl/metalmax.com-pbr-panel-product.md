Panel close-ups

The MetalMax PBR Panel, with its Purlin Bearing Ribs, will fasten to the substructure.It’s ideal for commercial or industrial applications because of its strength and because it can span over open framing.

- Coverage Width = 36”
- Major Rib Width = 12” On Center
- Major Rib Height = 1 ¼”
- Material = Galvalume® Coated Steel
- Available Gauges = 26, 24
- Installation Substrates = Open Framing or Solid Substrate
- Fasteners = Exposed
- Color / Finish = AC Galvalume, Weather XL, PVDF™
