# PBU PANEL

[![](https://metalmax.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/PBU-Panel-Rendering-1024x523.png)](https://metalmax.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/PBU-Panel-Rendering.png)

[FREE Estimate](https://metalmax.com/free-quote/)

[Panel Details](https://metalmax.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/MMRS-PBU-Panel-Sell-Sheet-3.20.26.pdf)

The PBU (Purlin Bearing Underside) Panel offers strength, secure alignment, and easy installation for roofing and siding. Its durable steel and WeatherXL™ finish ensure long-lasting color and corrosion resistance, making it ideal for commercial, agricultural, and industrial projects.

- Coverage Width = 36”
- Major Rib Width = 6” On Center
- Major Rib Height = 3/4”
- Available Gauges = 26, 24
- Material = Galvalume® Coated Steel
- Installation Substrates = Open Framing or Solid Substrate
- Fasteners = Exposed
- Color / Finish = Weather XL, PVDF™, AC Galvalume
