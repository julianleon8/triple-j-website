## MetalMax Roofing & Siding

MetalMax offers premium metal roofing and siding panels and trim in a wide variety of styles and colors to make your building look beautiful.From classic and low maintenance panels to more versatile panels, MetalMax is equipped for your needs. These panels can be used for roofs, siding, or sliding doors on residential, commercial, and agricultural buildings.MetalMax also supplies the related building supplies, accessories and contractor tools that will ensure your building systems function efficiently and that contractor jobs are accomplished quickly and safely.

Exposed Fastener Panels

EXPOSED FASTENER PANELS

An exposed fastener metal roof is a cost-effective system with quick installation.The panels are overlapped and screwed directly through the face of the panel into the roof deck using fasteners with gaskets to prevent leaking.

[Learn More](https://metalmax.com/exposed-fastener/)

STANDING SEAM PANELS

STANDING SEAM PANELS

A standing seam metal roof features concealed fasteners for a clean, modern appearance and enhanced weather resistance. The panels interlock vertically and are either snap-locked or mechanically seamed, making them ideal for residential and commercial buildings requiring long-lasting, low-maintenance performance.

[Learn More](https://metalmax.com/standing-seam/)

BOARD & BATTEN PANELS

BOARD & BATTEN PANELS

Board & Batten metal siding uses interlocking panels with concealed fasteners hidden behind raised battens for a traditional farmhouse look. With 16-inch coverage and durable steel construction, this siding offers a low-maintenance solution that blends classic style with modern performance.

[Learn More](https://metalmax.com/board-batten-panel/)

TRIM & RIDGE VENTS

TRIM & RIDGE VENTS

MetalMax offers a full range of metal trim and ridge vent systems designed to complement every panel style. Choose from Standing Seam Trim, R-panel Trim, and MaxPanel™ Trim, or request custom trim up to 26 feet. For ventilation, we supply FlexPro and Snap-Z systems to ensure airflow, energy efficiency, and long-term roof performance.

Metal roofing and siding is an excellent cost-effective alternative to asphalt shingle roofs as the it is permanent and will rarely need replacing. Consider equipping your residential, commercial, or agricultural space with steel roofing and siding. You will conserve energy in your home and leave a minimal environmental footprint on the planet.

POST-FRAME BUILDINGS & POLE BARNS

POST-FRAME BUILDINGS & POLE BARNS

Post-Frame construction is a versatile, cost-effective building method using large vertical posts instead of continuous foundations. This system allows for open interiors, quick assembly, and flexible design, making it ideal for barns, garages, retail spaces, and barndominiums. As demand grows for affordable, sustainable buildings, post-frame remains a top choice for residential, agricultural, and light commercial projects.

[Learn More](https://metalmax.com/building-systems/post-frame-buildings/)

Best for:

- Residential & Agricultural Applications
- Budget-friendly Construction
- Quick & Simple Installation (DIY-friendly)
- Flexible Design & Easy Future Expansion
- Attractive & Energy-efficient Design

COLD-FORMED STEEL BUILDINGS

COLD-FORMED STEEL BUILDINGS

Cold Formed steel buildings (CFSBs) use steel shaped at room temperature to form strong, durable structural components. They’re popular for residential and commercial use, including Barn-dominiums, because they offer design flexibility and structural integrity combined with shorter production lead-times.
