# Triple J FB Group Targets — Working Research Doc (2026-05-01)

Companion to `dev/sales-pack-2026-04-30.md` Variants 1–5. This is the **where to post**, not the **what to post**.

## Method

- Reasoning-first from market context: Bell County base, military overlap (Cavazos), ranch/ag adjacency, Hispanic community, RV/boat owners on Stillhouse + Belton lakes, homeowners in subdivisions.
- **Tavily research skipped** — most FB groups are closed/private and don't index to the open web. Ground truth is inside FB while logged in. Re-evaluate Tavily only if this list yields <15 viable groups after verification.
- Specific group names below are **search terms**, not guaranteed exact names. FB's search returns several variations of every theme — Julian picks the live one with the highest member count.

## Per-group verification checklist (run while logged into FB)

For every candidate group below, before posting:

1. **Member count** — >5K = high-leverage · 1K–5K = niche but viable · <1K = skip unless ultra-targeted
2. **Pinned rules post** — explicitly allows service-business posts? Admin-approval queue? Or "no advertising" outright?
3. **Recent activity** — at least 1 new post per day in the last 7 days = active feed
4. **Audience match** — local + in Triple J's buyer profile (homeowner, RV owner, rancher, milspouse, Hispanic homeowner)
5. **Admin posture** — can you DM the admin to ask permission first? Often opens doors that direct posting closes.
6. **Spanish-language fit (Variant 5)** — is content bilingual or English-only?

## Scoring shorthand

- 🟢 **Post immediately** — known service-friendly or low admin friction
- 🟡 **Try with caution** — DM admin first or watch for removal after first post
- 🔴 **Skip** — strict anti-business rules or off-target audience
- ❓ **Verify on FB** — group name/existence not confirmed
