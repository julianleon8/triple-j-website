## Part B: Who should the Page follow?

### B1. Strategy

- The Page currently follows nobody — that's a cold-start signal that hurts algorithm trust.
- Following is **public** (visible on the Page profile under "Following"). Curate accordingly.
- Pages can follow up to ~5,000 other Pages; the bar is "would I be comfortable a customer or competitor saw this on our profile?"
- **Reciprocity:** many local Pages auto-follow back. Following first creates mutual visibility for free.
- **Algorithmic effect:** following local civic/municipal/Chamber Pages signals "local business" to Meta's classifier and helps Triple J surface in local-search results.

### B2. Don't follow

- ❌ **Direct competitors** — Big Tex Metal Buildings, ProStructures of Belton, Capital Metal Buildings, Krystal Skye Barker, Laura Torres / Laura Alicia Torres Fuentes, Chris Quinn, Eagle Carports, Get Carports, Carport Central. Following competitors publicly signals weakness.
- ❌ **Suppliers by name** — per CLAUDE.md supplier-agnostic positioning, do not publicly identify Triple J's steel suppliers. Following them on the Page is a public identification.
- ❌ **Politically charged Pages** of any direction — alienates ~50% of any audience instantly.
- ❌ **Anything off-brand** — adult content, MLM, get-rich-quick. Public follow list is a brand signal.

### B4. Ongoing follow rules (post-initial 25)

Once the cold-start is solved, add Pages on these triggers:

- **Customer with a Page** — every closed job, check if the customer has a business Page. Follow it. Reciprocal goodwill.
- **Engaged commenter** — when someone repeatedly engages with Triple J's posts (likes 3+ posts or comments), check if they have a business Page; follow it.
- **Adjacent local trades** — local non-competing trades (concrete delivery, ready-mix, equipment rental, fencing, electrical, HVAC, plumbing, roofing) → 5–10 across Bell + McLennan. Builds the local-trades cluster on FB.
- **Strategic GC + custom builders** — the GCs from `dev/sales-pack-2026-04-30.md` §6 (CRW Construction, Wright Builders, Emerson Construction, Carothers Executive Homes, Flintrock Builders) → follow their Pages as a soft-pitch signal that Triple J is paying attention.
- **Past-customer brands** if they're business owners — ranch operations, landscaping outfits, small commercial.

### B5. What to expect

- ~30–50% follow-back rate from local civic/Chamber/realtor Pages within 7 days.
- ~10–20% follow-back from Cavazos / military Pages (those Pages are managed by orgs that don't reciprocate freely).
- Local-business algorithmic signal kicks in after ~10 local civic follows — Pages with "follows the city it operates in + the Chamber" rank better in local-search "metal building near me"–type queries.
