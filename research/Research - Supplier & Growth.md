# Supplier Partnerships & Growth Channels — Triple J Metal LLC
*Last updated: 2026-04-15 via NotebookLM*

## MetalMax Partnership
- **PBR panels:** 36-inch coverage, most versatile residential/commercial panel
- **PBU panels:** alternate profile, similar applications
- **WeatherXL™ color system:** UV resistance + corrosion protection; 26 & 29 gauge options
- **Galvalume® substrate:** base metal for long-term rust prevention
- **Drip Stop:** anti-condensation felt applied to panel underside — moisture control in barns/shops
- **Sheffield line:** 26-gauge only; concealed-fastener options (MaxLoc, MaxSeam, MaxSnap)

## Public Procurement — CMBL
- **Texas Centralized Master Bidders List (CMBL):** $70/year registration
- **Relevant NIGP codes:** steel erection, welding, fabricated metal structures
- Opens access to state agency, county, and municipality contracts
- **Action:** Register before next fiscal year (September in Texas)
