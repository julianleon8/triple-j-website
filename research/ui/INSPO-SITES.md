### 1. WolfSteel Buildings ⭐ PRIMARY DESIGN TARGET
**URL:** https://wolfsteelbuildings.com
**Owner verdict:** "Theirs is the one I like the most but I think we could do better."

**Their full service stack (Triple J matches ALL of these):**
- Red Iron Metal Buildings (ONLY — no tubular)
- Permitting
- Concrete & Site Preparation
- Insulation
- Gutter Systems
- Custom Accessories

**Their design breakdown:**
- White body, black header, logo top-left, phone top-right, "Start Building Here" CTA
- H1: "Revamping Industry Standards" — one bold confident statement, not a keyword-stuffed description
- Hero background video of an actual Texas build
- "From Dirt to Finish" section with embedded YouTube videos — educational content = trust
- Projects page: each job is its own entry titled "WIDTHxLENGTH [Description] in [City], TX [ZIP]"
- Concrete page headline: "We Don't Cut Corners, We Pour Them" — clever positioning
- "Select areas of Texas ONLY" — scarcity framing

**Where Triple J beats them:**
- 48-hour build (WolfSteel takes 24–48 hrs just to send a quote callback)
- Lower price point — WolfSteel is premium branded, we're best value in Central TX
- One call, one contract — theirs splits building + concrete as separate services
- Our backyard is Temple/Harker Heights/Fort Cavazos — they're spread thin across TX

**Where we build a better site:**
- Their projects page is a basic WordPress grid — ours should be richer cards with dimensions + city
- Their hero video loads slow — ours should be a strong static photo, instant load
- No trust/stats strip above the fold — we put "150+ Projects · Built in 48 Hours · Temple TX" right there
- Their phone is top-right only — ours should be impossible to miss on mobile too

## DESIGN DIRECTION SUMMARY

Based on research, here's the recommended vibe for Triple J:


**Mood:** Dark industrial → clean white body → blue accents
- Hero: black or near-black background, white headline, blue CTA
- Body sections: white bg, gray-50 alternating
- Accents: blue (bring back the exact hex/shade you want — navy, royal, steel blue, or bright blue)
- Secondary: black for backgrounds, headers, footers

**Typography:**
- Headline: **Barlow Condensed Bold** or **Oswald** — heavy, industrial, free on Google Fonts
- Body: Geist (already installed) or Inter
- Look at WolfSteel's "Revamping Industry Standards" headline — that weight and confidence is the target

**Layout pattern (homepage):**
1. Dark hero — H1 + subline + dual CTA + trust strip
2. White stats bar — 150+ Projects · 48-Hr Build · Turnkey Concrete · Temple TX
3. Services grid — Carports, Garages, Barns, RV Covers (4 cards)
4. Why Triple J — welded vs bolted explainer (this is your biggest differentiator)
5. How it works — 3 steps: Call → We prep → We build in 48hrs
6. Service areas — 5 city cards linking to location pages
7. Quote form — full width CTA with inline form
8. Footer — dark, phone + address + city links

**Phone number rule (stolen from every top site):** Phone number must be visible in the header at all times, on every device, without scrolling. This alone drives more calls than any other design decision.
