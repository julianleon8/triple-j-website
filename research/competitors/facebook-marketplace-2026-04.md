## Takeaways for Triple J

1. **The Marketplace bar is low.** Placeholder pricing and one-line titles dominate. A listing with real photos, itemized pricing, and specs will outperform 90% of what's there.
2. **ProStructures is the pace car.** Their $33,855 turn-key 30x50 concrete+garage package is the number to beat or match. Triple J should have a directly comparable published package.
3. **Copy what works, keep what's ours.** Take their format (itemized breakdown, specs list, wind rating, anchor price) — keep the Triple J differentiators (welded red iron, local install crew, same-week turnaround, 20+ years).
4. **Run our own Marketplace listings.** Temple/Belton Marketplace is a lead channel our competitors are under-serving. A weekly rotation of listings (carport, garage, RV cover, shop) with real prices and real photos would pull leads immediately.
5. **Counter the "free concrete slab" framing.** ProStructures bundles concrete into the anchor discount. Triple J should decide whether to match, beat, or differentiate (e.g., "we pour our own slabs — no subcontractor coordination").
