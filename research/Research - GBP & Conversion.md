# GBP & Conversion Optimization — Triple J Metal LLC
*Last updated: 2026-04-15 via NotebookLM*

## Google Business Profile (GBP) Strategy

### Why GBP Matters
GBP acts as a **"Digital Tiebreaker"** — when GCs or homeowners are evaluating two similar contractors, the one with a stronger GBP profile wins the call.

### Current Standard (what competitors are doing)
- 30–100+ reviews
- Active job site photos showing equipment + completed structures
- Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across all directories

### GBP Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Verify physical location in Temple (primary)
- [ ] Add service-area business (SAB) radius to capture: Belton, Killeen, Harker Heights, Copperas Cove, Waco
- [ ] Maintain NAP consistency across: GBP, Yelp, HomeAdvisor, Angi, BBB, Facebook, website footer
- [ ] Post weekly job photos (before/during/after — use skid steer shots)
- [ ] Target **review velocity**: 2–4 new reviews per month minimum
- [ ] Review sentiment to mention: "same week," "welded," "concrete included," "Temple," "local"
- [ ] Respond to every review (shows active business)

### Review Acquisition
- Ask on job completion — text the customer a direct GBP review link
- Follow up 48 hours post-job via SMS
- Offer a small thank-you (branded merch, referral credit) for leaving a review

## Website Conversion Optimization

### What Moves the Needle for Texas Contractors
1. **Niche landing pages** outperform generic "metal buildings" pages
   - "Barndominium foundations" page vs. homepage
   - "RV carports" vs. generic gallery
   - Each page targets a specific search intent

2. **Localized technical authority signals:**
   - Mention 4,000 PSI concrete for Blackland Prairie clay
   - Reference Bell County permit process
   - Use city names in headings, not just meta tags

3. **Trust badges — highest conversion drivers:**
   - "Licensed & Insured"
   - "BBB A+ Rated" (once obtained)
   - "Veteran-Owned" (if applicable)
   - "HomeAdvisor 4.7 Stars" (currently have this)

### Quote Form Best Practices
- Multi-step form reduces abandonment vs. single long form
- Step 1: Project type + size (high intent, easy to answer)
- Step 2: Location + timeline (qualifies the lead)
- Step 3: Contact info (earned after engagement)
- Show progress bar
- Reassurance copy near submit: "No commitment. We'll call within 24 hours."
